# Code of Conduct

Thinkroom follows the [Contributor Covenant, version
2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Contributors are expected to make participation respectful and
harassment-free, give and accept constructive feedback, focus on what is best
for the community, and show empathy toward other participants. Harassment,
discrimination, personal attacks, publishing another person's private
information, and other conduct that would reasonably be considered
inappropriate are not acceptable.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates these
standards. Enforcement reports should be sent privately to the maintainers
through the repository's **Security** tab. Reports will be reviewed promptly
and handled with appropriate confidentiality.
